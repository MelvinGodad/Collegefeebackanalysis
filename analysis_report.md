# Student Feedback Analysis Report

## Executive Summary
This report analyzes student feedback from recent campus events to measure satisfaction and identify areas for improvement.

## Key Insights
- **Overall Satisfaction**: Satisfaction varies significantly by event type.
- **Sentiment Analysis**:
    - **Positive**: A significant portion of feedback is positive, often highlighting food and categorization.
    - **Negative**: Common complaints include "Speaker was inaudible" and "Started very late".
    - **Neutral**: Many students felt events were "Okay" or "Decent".

## Recommendations
1.  **Audio Quality**: Multiple complaints about inaudible speakers suggest we need better sound equipment or pre-checks.
2.  **Punctuality**: "Started very late" was a recurring negative comment. We should enforce stricter schedules.
3.  **Venue Management**: "Venue was too hot" appeared in the feedback; ensure proper ventilation/AC for future events.
